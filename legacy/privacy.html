We don't speak legalese, so let's keep things simple: As a FOSS-centered community, we can assure you that your data will not:

  • Be collected and/or stored
  • Be sold to third parties
  • Processed or transferred

In case that we do have some sort of personal data that you want us to delete (for example from a service we host) you can contact us on Matrix or Discord and we will try to do everythign that's on our hands to delete such data.
