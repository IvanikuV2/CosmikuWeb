Cosmiku is a small FOSS-centered community made by people like you, similar to envs.net but less professional. Everything is self-hosted so we have total control over everything that goes through it.

If someone sent you this link, this is your invite. Come in! We currently talk through two chat platforms, Discord and Matrix. Psst, both are fully bridged ;)
